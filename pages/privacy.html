Welcome to ROJGAR. Your privacy is critically important to us. This document outlines the types of personal information collected by ROJGAR and how it is used to empower your digital journey.

When you visit our marketplace or purchase a digital asset, we collect:

  • Personal Identity: Name, email, and billing details for secure transaction processing.

  • Transaction Execution: Delivering high-impact digital products to your inbox.
  • Service Enhancement: Improving our support response times and marketplace UI.

3. Security Protocol

We implement elite security measures to protect your data. All transactions are handled through encrypted SSL technology to ensure your financial safety.

4. Support Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries, reach out to our legal team at legal@rojgar.com.
